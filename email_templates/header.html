Enjoy your amazing local streams responsibly! If you are fishing the Eagle and Upper Colorado Rivers, please be aware of water temps at your destination.

When temperatures reach concern levels ( > 65°F) don't play or fight fish and unhook and return them quickly to the water. Anticipate further warming in the afternoon and consider stopping for the day before temperatures exceed 70°F. Fishing during very warm water events is unhealthy for trout and can actually be lethal to the fish.
